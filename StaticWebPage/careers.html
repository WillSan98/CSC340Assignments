Positions:

Card Grader

Description:

As a card grader, your duties will involve meticulously examining cards before distribution sends for shipping. The trust of our customers relies on us shipping all cards as listed on the product listing. All singles must be inspected for creases, water damages, scratches, peeling, and/or misprinting. This position will including bending over a desk for long periods of time while using occular acuity to examine cards. Eye strain may come with extended examination.

Qualifications:
  • Strong Eyesight
  • Experience in card grading and the ability to work under pressure
  • Experience with a large of variety of card games
  • Ability to quickly learn new printing patterns and common misprints
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively with colleagues

Product Packer

Description:

As a product packer, you will ensure all orders are shipped by Atomic Gaming TCG standards. You will be the last point of contact with merchandise before it reaches our clientel. You assume full responiblity for upholding the value we bring to our customers. The position does come with some menial labor so do not expect this to be a desk job.

Qualifications:
  • Ability to lift at least 20lbs
  • Ability to work under pressure. Especially during periods of high order volume.
  • Experience with a large of variety of card games
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively with colleagues
