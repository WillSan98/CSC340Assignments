About Us:

Atomic Gaming TCG is an online card market established in 2016 by its founder John Smith. Initially a local LGS owner, John thought to bring the joy of card games past his small town of Nowhere, Indiana. Since then John's vision has been expanded into a country wide marketplace that provides every trading card game that localized to United States. Here at Atomic Gaming TCG we understand that quality and expediency are valued by our customer so we strive to make sure each purchase meets our high standards. Whether it's Pokemon, Yugion, or even niches like FFTC, we care all of your trading card game desires.
